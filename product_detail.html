Cardboard Signs are the perfect option for the environmentally conscious buyer looking for indoor signage. Our Cardboard Signs have a smooth flat surface that is printed in high resolution, full colour on our HP Latex R2000 printer using water based inks that provide glossier prints with great scratch resistance. The 3mm or 5mm Cardboard material is fluted, giving it great strength while remaining lightweight, and is proudly FSC certified so you can be rest assured that you can be sure that you are making an environmentally sustainable choice.

  • Printed in amazing full colour using water based Latex print technology
  • Made from an FSC certified material
  • Fully recyclable
  • Single or double sided printing
  • 3mm or 5mm thickness options available
  • Can be cut to convex geometric shapes including circles, triangles and hexagons
  • Available in any size from 100mm x 100mm up to 2415mm x 1200mm
  • Larger sizes can be made up with multiple panels

Shipping prices calculated for this product are based on bubblewrap packaging as well as palletising. Panels larger than 1200mm height or width will need to be palletised to ensure their safe delivery if sent outside of Sydney Metro. For customers in the Sydney Metro area we deliver these larger panels using a same-day, door to door courier service to minimise the possibility of it being damaged.
