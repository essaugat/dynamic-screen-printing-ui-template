Our Services

Here are our Services. We are best at what we do.

01

detail & soft feel

Direct to garments

Direct to Garment or DTG, offers more flexibility and higher colour resolution. With out the need for screens, has application from short runs to thousands and quick turnaround times. Generally suited to cotton rich garments from one of our trusted suppliers.
02

full site

Digital transfer

Our digital transfer applications allow us the versatility to adapt extremely complex designs and images. Combine this with a full suite of digital materials and fusion bond presses and we can move quickly to complete your project. Our digital transfers complement our other decoration processes at Dynamic.
03

THE CORE BUSINESS

Screenprinting

Screenprinting is the core of our business and we have the latest in screenprinting technology. When you select a supplier of screenprinting services, you are placing your trust, your reputation and your brand with your decorator. At Dynamic we have skilled employees, the right equipment, comprehensive range of inks and the right attitude to care about your job.
04

LATEST GENERATION

Supcolour

Supacolour is the latest technology in digital reproduction of logos. Supacolour offers ultra-high detail and unlimited colours with high resolution. Dynamic Screenprinting is your authorised decorator for Supacolour and we have the complete range of fusion bond presses that allow control and consistency.
05

PRESENTATION & PACKAGING

Finished product

We can provide a range of solutions that will make your finished product stand out. From our new degradable poly bags, to custom labelling and plain package boxing.
06

COMPLETE UNDERSTANDING

Delivery

Your order is only complete when it arrives on time at the final destination and in great shape - we understand the delivery cycle from order to completion and use your preferred carrier or we can arrange delivery from our well known fleet.
